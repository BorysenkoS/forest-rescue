About the App

"Forest Rescue" is a match-3 puzzle game set in a beautiful forest where players help adorable animals save their home from an evil lumberjack. The game blends classic match-3 mechanics with a rich storyline, offering a compelling mix of puzzles and adventure.

As you progress through the game, you`ll solve puzzles to collect resources and complete tasks that will restore the forest to its former glory.

With stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and regular updates, "Forest Rescue" is perfect for players who love puzzles with a purpose.
